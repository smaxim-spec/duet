# Adult IUL Call Script
## 5-Stage Framework for IUL Conversations (Retirement / Infinite Banking / Lifetime Income)

---

## Stage 1: The Connection (The Disarm & Identify)
**Goal:** Lower resistance, identify core objective: Retirement, Infinite Banking, or Lifetime Income Rider.

### Checkpoints:
1. Reference the request/appointment or walk-in
2. Ask: looking for anything specific or exploring options?
3. Lower status — just seeing eligibility
4. KEY: Identify their goal — death benefit, tax-free retirement, infinite banking, or lifetime income rider?

## Stage 2: The Engagement (Building the Gap)
5. Walk me through current retirement strategy (401k, IRA, etc.)
6. How long have you been contributing?
7. Do you like that your 401k will be fully taxed at retirement?
8. Clarify: "how do you mean exactly?"
9. What if market crashes 30-40% right before retirement AND you still pay taxes?
10. Are you willing to settle for that?
11. Before today, had you been looking for crash-proof, tax-free strategies?
12. If approved: tax-free growth, market protection, liquidity without penalties — what would that do?
13. What happens if you keep the 401k, taxes go up, market wipes out half your nest egg?
14. Do you want to put yourself in that situation?

## Stage 3: The Transition
15. Summarize: want to [retire/bank/income] + money stuck in taxable, market-exposed 401k
16. Acknowledge the worry
17. I have an option that fits

## Stage 4: The Presentation (tailored to their goal)
18. Name it: Indexed Universal Life / IUL
19. Structured for max cash accumulation — minimum death benefit
20. Growth tied to market index — upside
21. 0% floor — no losses in crashes
22. [Retirement/Banking path]: tax-free loans, money keeps compounding
23. [Income Rider path]: guaranteed lifetime income, can't outlive money
24. Does that make sense? / Do you see how this helps?

## Stage 5: The Commitment
25. Do you feel this could be the answer?
26. Why do you feel like it is though?
27. First step: medical approval
28. Start collecting info — into application
