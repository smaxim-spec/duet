CALLEY PHONE SYNC TROUBLESHOOTING
===================================
Last updated: 2026-03-31

PROBLEM: Leads pushed from Duet show on Calley website but NOT on the phone app.

FIX (Step by step):
1. Open Calley app on phone
2. Go to Settings → Log Out
3. Force close the Calley app (swipe away from app switcher)
4. Reopen Calley app
5. Log back in with your credentials
6. The "Duet" list should now appear with leads

IF THAT DOESN'T WORK:
- Delete and reinstall the Calley app on your phone
- Check Calley website → make sure the "Duet" list status is "Active" (not "Pending")
- Pull to refresh the list screen in the mobile app
- Make sure your Calley subscription (Pro Plan) is active

PUSH WORKFLOW (from Duet):
1. Go to My CRM tab
2. Use filters to select which leads you want to push
3. Click the orange "Push to Calley" bar
4. Adjust the count if needed, then confirm
5. After push completes, do the phone sync steps above if list doesn't appear

SYNC RESULTS BACK (from Calley to Duet):
1. After finishing calls in Calley app, go to My CRM tab
2. Click "Sync Calley" button in the orange bar
3. Duet will pull call results and update dispositions/stages automatically

  Appt Set       →  Appt Set
  Quoted         →  Quoted

  Bad Number     →  Bad #
  Lost           →  Lost

NOTE: Calley sometimes spells "Nurturing" as "Nuturing" (no 'r').
Both spellings are handled in Duet's mapping.
