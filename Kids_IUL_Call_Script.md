# Kids IUL Call Script
## 5-Stage Framework for Child IUL Wealth-Building Conversations

---

## Stage 1: The Connection (The Disarm)
**Goal:** Lower guard, find out what they want for their kids.

### Checkpoints:
1. Reference the booked appointment / calendar
2. Ask: looking for anything specific or just exploring options?
3. Lower status — just seeing eligibility
4. Ask permission to ask questions about what's in place

## Stage 2: The Engagement (Building the Gap)
**Goal:** Expose flaws in current savings (inflation, taxes, restrictions), envision tax-free head start.

### Checkpoints:
5. Walk me through what you have in place for the child
6. How long have you been saving?
7. Do you like the returns on the bank account?
8. Impact on giving them a head start at 18-20?
9. Why IUL vs 529 college plan?
10. Before today, had you been looking for flexible growth strategies?
11. Paint the picture: tax-free growth, market protection, use for college/house/business
12. As a parent, what would that do for you personally?
13. What happens if savings lose to inflation and child starts with debt?
14. Do you want to put them in that situation?

## Stage 3: The Transition
15. Summarize: want flexible tax-free nest egg + money stuck in bank losing value
16. Acknowledge worry about child starting with debt
17. I have an option that fits

## Stage 4: The Presentation
18. Name it: Indexed Universal Life / IUL
19. Structured for maximum cash accumulation — minimum death benefit
20. Growth tied to market index — upside participation
21. 0% floor — market crashes, no losses
22. Unlike 529: tax-free loans for college, house, or business
23. Money stays and keeps compounding
24. Does that make sense?

## Stage 5: The Commitment
25. Do you feel this could give them that head start?
26. Why do you feel like it is though?
27. Next steps: application to lock in insurability while young and healthy
28. Start collecting info
